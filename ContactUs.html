Who we are?

Galilee Freight is a moving company based in Auckland, New Zealand that offers residential, commercial, and long distance moving services. Their experienced team ensures a stress-free and smooth move, and they also provide short-term and long-term storage solutions. To get a quote, you can contact them directly or fill out an online form with as much information as possible.

What is included in the cost of a move?

The cost of a move typically includes labor, packing materials, and transportation of your belongings. Additional services like packing and unpacking, loading and unloading, and storage may also be included. Be sure to ask about any extra fees for heavy or fragile items. Insurance is not provided, so it's best to speak with your own insurance company about coverage.

What happens if something gets damaged or lost during the move?

Galilee Freight removals are at the owner's risk, so it's important to take detailed inventory of your belongings and document their condition before the move to make it easier to identify any damages that may occur. Please see their Additional Services & Conditions page for more detail.
