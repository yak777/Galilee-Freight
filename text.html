FAQs

How do I get a quote for my move?

You can get a quote for your move by contacting us directly or by filling out an online quote form on our website. Be sure to provide as much information as possible, including the size of your home, the distance of the move, and any special requests or considerations you may have.

What is included in the cost of a move?

The cost of a move typically includes some if not all depending on requirements, the cost of labor, packing materials, and transportation of your belongings. It may also include additional services such as packing and unpacking, loading and unloading, and storage. Be sure to ask about any additional fees that may apply, such as for heavy or fragile items. Insurance is not provided, please speak with your Insurance company about possible cover.

What happens if something gets damaged or lost during the move?

Galilee Freight removals are at the owner's risk. Please see out Additional Services & Conditions page for more detail. It is also a good idea to take detailed inventory of your belongings and document their condition before the move to make it easier to identify any damages that may occur.
